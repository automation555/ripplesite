RippleDeals is helping to develop rippled, an open source payment routing server, to find trust chains and payment paths between customers of banks, barter clubs, community currencies, online accounting software, and others.
RippleDeals and rippled are based on earlier code, also open source, at ripplepay.com.
When rippled is mature, RippleDeals hopes to be the first rippled client.
